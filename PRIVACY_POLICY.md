## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Topicx ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
When you register and use Topicx, we collect:
- **Account Information:** Username, full name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture (optional), bio
- **Contact Information:** Email for account recovery and notifications

### 2.2 User-Generated Content
- **Posts and Comments:** Text content, images you share
- **Messages:** Private messages, group chat messages, voice messages
- **Groups:** Groups you create or join
- **Social Interactions:** Friend connections, followers, following, likes

### 2.3 Location Data (Optional)
- **Voluntary Sharing:** You can choose to share your location in group posts
- **Road Status Alerts:** Location-based traffic, accident, or road condition reports
- **Usage:** Location is ONLY shared when you explicitly choose to do so
- **Control:** You can revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings

### 2.4 Audio Data (Optional)
- **Voice Messages:** Audio recordings you voluntarily send in chats and groups
- **Storage:** Voice messages are stored on our servers
- **Access:** Only intended recipients can access your voice messages

### 2.5 Device Information
- **Device Type:** Mobile device model, operating system version
- **App Usage:** Features used, crash reports for debugging
- **Network Information:** IP address for security purposes

### 2.6 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, features used
- **Performance Data:** App crashes, load times (for improving service)
- **Authentication Tokens:** For maintaining your session

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Public Information
The following information is PUBLIC by default:
- Your username, full name, and profile picture
- Posts and comments you make (unless in private groups)
- Your friend list, followers, and following list
- Groups you create or join

### 4.2 Within Groups
- Group posts, messages, and voice messages are shared with all group members
- Location data shared in groups is visible to all members

### 4.3 We Do NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your data for advertising purposes
- ❌ Use your data for marketing by third parties
- ❌ Track your location without explicit permission

### 4.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, such as:
- Complying with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Protecting the rights and safety of our users
- Investigating fraud or security issues

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Holds:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law
- **Backups:** Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days

## 6. Your Rights and Controls

### 6.1 Access and Control
- **View Data:** Access your profile and posts anytime
- **Edit Information:** Update your profile, email, or password
- **Delete Content:** Delete your posts, comments, and messages
- **Delete Account:** Permanently delete your account and data

### 6.2 Privacy Controls
- **Location Permissions:** Enable/disable in device settings
- **Microphone Permissions:** Control voice message access
- **Notifications:** Manage notification preferences in app settings

### 6.3 Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
If applicable, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update incorrect information
- **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Objection:** Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: isyerimiz@gmail.com

## 7. Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt
- **Secure Transmission:** HTTPS for all data transfers
- **Access Controls:** Limited staff access to user data
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute force attacks
- **XSS Protection:** Input sanitization to prevent malicious code

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
