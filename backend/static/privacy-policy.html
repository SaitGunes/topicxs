With your permission, TopicX may access your device's camera or photo library so you can upload images with your posts or profile.

  • We only access photos or media that you choose to upload.

1.4 Location Information

TopicX does not require your precise location to function. If in the future we offer location-based features and you choose to enable them, we will clearly ask for your permission and will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

If you access TopicX through a web interface, we may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
